## About New Think

New Think is a consulting and ideas brand built around one belief: AI can and should be used for almost everything — if you think differently about the task or issue.

This site documents real-world AI use cases across a wide variety of everyday and professional problems. Some posts are quick hits. Some are longer stories of a problem and how AI solved it.

The goal is simple: show what's actually possible, not just what's theoretically possible.
